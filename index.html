How it works

Accountability, built into the phone itself

01

Pair by consent

You and your partner both install Anchor and exchange a simple code. No one is ever added secretly, and either of you can unpair at any time.

02

Block by default

Pick the apps that stay open: your essentials. Everything else is blocked behind a shield, including apps you install later and the browser.

03

Unlock together

Need a blocked app? Send a request. Your partner approves or denies it from their phone, for a time you both agree on. Temptation becomes a conversation.

Not a spy app

Mutual by design

  • Both partners see the same dashboard. There is no hidden "watcher" role.
  • Pairing requires both people to opt in by exchanging a code in person.
  • Either person can unpair at any time, and the other is always notified.
  • Everything Anchor does is disclosed in our privacy policy. No ads, no data sales, no tracking.
